About us
We are a coffee shop with a passion not only for good coffee but also for dogs and making their lives better! By coming into the cafe you support us taking care of our dogs and if you happen to click with one of them, you can adopt them!
You can reserve a coffee date with a specific dog that you can read about below or just come in and enjoy our delicious drinks, that you can check out in the menu.
